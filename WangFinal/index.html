Biographies

Multidisciplinary Artists with strong interest in Asian, European, and Middle East culture. Confident in using material such as oil paint, ink, and digital software to create works. Specialize in environment design, concept design, and symbolic painting. Currently exploring book making and artist's book. Conducted online artwork commisions.

Based in San Diego, United States

Artist's statement

My interest in art is to explore both the mental world of our mind and the physical world where relationships with others are built. I use symbolic elements drawn from traditional culture, folklore, and superstition to illustrate topics that we can still struggle with nowadays: fearing loss, finding love, or trying to remain at peace.

Chuan.no
Oil Painting

Oil paint is currently my main medium in creating art. I admire the patience and care required in the process of using oil. I also find using this medium rewarding as it reflects truthfully about the state of the painter. It sometimes reflects subtle emotions that I had but was not aware of.

Calligraphy

I use Calligraphy for both traditional writing and contemporary abstraction. When doing calligraphy, each stroke and direction of the brushworks convey subtle attitude. It is the art of finding unity within self.
